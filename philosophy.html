Philosophy

TO EXCEED YOUR EXPECTATIONS

Amanda has five unbreakable rules:

• Customer Service: The salon is built around outstanding customer service, paying attention to your individual needs 
• Punctuality: At Amanda Touch of Color, you won't be kept waiting, and each client receives undivided attention
• Individuality: Rather than stick to the same generic styles, Amanda treats each client as an individual, finding a style and color that suits their facial structure, complexion, preferences, and personality
• Comfort: The salon is not just a place for hair coloring and styling: it is a place to soak up the friendly atmosphere in comfortable surroundings
• No Compromise: Amanda uses only the finest products and constantly strives to reach new levels of perfection. As an artist, she wants every client to leave the salon with a smile

CUT, COLOR, STYLE, COMFORT

For many, visiting a hair salon isn't just about the styling and coloring, but a way to relax amongst friends, taking a break from the stresses and strains of modern life. Amanda Touch of Color Salon isn't just about just our skill, but about the whole experience in surroundings that reflect the warmth of her personality. 

This simple philosophy and Amanda's insistence on the personal touch is why our clients are happy to refer us to their friends. At Amanda Touch of Color Salon, the unique blend of artistry and relaxation means that you will want to keep coming back, time and time again
