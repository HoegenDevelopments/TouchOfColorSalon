Welcome!

Amanda Touch of Color Salon is a salon that is located in the Laurelhurst neighborhood of Seattle. We specialize in hair coloring, styling, and cutting. We prioritize customer satisfaction and happiness above all, and strive to make things as relaxing as possible.

At Amanda Touch of Color Salon, we offer a variety of services, including hair coloring, styling, and cutting.
